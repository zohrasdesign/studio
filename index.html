About Zohra's Design

At Zohra´s Design, we believe that organization shouldn´t be complicated or stressfull. That´s why we created simple, clean, beautifull and practical digital planners and trackers designed to help you take control of your day and your goals-without overwhelm. Our products are made with care for people who want to stay positive but don´t have time for complicated systems. Whether you´re just starting your planning journey or looking for easy tools to keep you on track, we´re here to help you make everyday count. Thank you for trusting Zohra´s Design to be part of your organization journey!
